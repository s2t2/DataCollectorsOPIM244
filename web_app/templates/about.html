About

By 2022, 90% of corporate strategies will explicitly mention information as a critical enterprise asset and analytics as an essential competency. - Gartner

Innovo Web Apps is a suite of publicly available, business intelligence tools. Our mission is to provide high quality data and analytics tools to small businesses and student consultants. Our tools include:


  • Yelp Data Aggregator: collect data on 50 local businesses for competitor analysis.
  • Twitter Data Aggregator: collect data on 500 tweets for social media analysis.
  • New York Times Data Aggregator: collect data on 100 news articles for industry analysis.
  • USA Census Dashboard: visualize industry growth, firm sizes, and competition.
  • Financial Statements: income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement. Financial ratios are calculated automatically.
  • Marketing Templates: Excel file to organize social media strategy and post templates.

This site was created by Innovo Consulting, a pro-bono, student-run consulting agency operating at Georgetown University.

Innovo Web Apps was founded by Will Nemoy, a junior at Georgetown's business school and an incoming Business Intelligence Engineer at Amazon. Innovo would like to thank the following students for their significant contributions:
